WELCOME TO THE CLUB!

Hello everyone! My name is Rebecca. Please let me be the first to welcome you to this QUEEN fan club!! Although the club is fairly new, we are so excited to share what benefits being a member can bring to you!

When you join this QUEEN Fan Club, you will receive TWO full color magazines a year! Each will have a handwritten letter from a member of the band and contain a host of photos, all the latest information on what QUEEN and the individual members are doing, quizzes, pen pals, collectors information and a lots of other features.

Your membership also includes access to our members only online section, which features exclusive content, gallery, chatroom and forum. All our events are also for members only, so there is a sense of comraderie, and you know you will be surrounded by people that love QUEEN just as much as you do!
